Tom And Jerry

Tom and Jerry is a classic animated series featuring the comedic rivalry between Tom, a cat, and Jerry, a mouse. Debuting in 1940, the show is known for its slapstick humor, minimal dialogue, and clever antics. It remains a beloved, iconic cartoon, entertaining audiences with timeless, physical comedy.
